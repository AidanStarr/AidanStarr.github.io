About me:
I'm a Paleoceanography Research Associate working in Prof. Ian Hall's lab at Cardiff University. I trained as an oceanographer (MSci from Southampton University) before turning my interest towards ocean-climate interactions during the Plio-Pleistocene. I have recently submitted my PhD thesis titled `Surface and Deep Hydrography across the Mid-Pleistocene Transition; Multi-proxy paleoceanographic reconstructions from the Southwest Indian Ocean'.
